WELCOME TO
UDUAK-ABASI 2027

UduakAbasi 2027 is a forward-looking political movement in Etinan Federal Constituency—covering Etinan, Nsit Ibom, and Nsit Ubium LGAs—championed by Emem Jackson in his bid to represent the people in the Federal House of Representatives. The group is built on the principles of inclusive leadership, youth empowerment, community development, and effective representation. It seeks to unite the constituency, amplify the voices of the people, and drive meaningful progress through responsive governance, strategic initiatives, and a shared vision for a better future.

THE LEADER:

Hon. Emem Jackson

Meet the leader of the UDUAK-ABASI 2027 Hon. Emem Jackson a seasoned entrepreneur, resilient comrade, and visionary leader whose unwavering commitment to service has earned him widespread respect across communities. A proud graduate of the prestigious University of Port Harcourt, he has distinguished himself as a politician, administrator, and passionate advocate for the people.
